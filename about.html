About Us

JHUND is a talent discovery app for creative projects. You can search for talent profiles directly on Instagram using prompts in the JHUND APP. Once you find suitable profiles, invite them to collaborate by forming JHUNDs (project groups) where you can chat and manage the project.

Our MARKETPLACE enables creators to post listings related to the creative industry. Interested leads can connect directly with the sellers. Whether you're a musician, designer, filmmaker, or dancer — JHUND helps unite and empower creators to collaborate effortlessly.
