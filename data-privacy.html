Data Privacy & Account Deletion

At The Jhund, your privacy is a top priority. This page outlines how your data is managed and the steps you can take to request deletion of your account and associated data.

What Data We Collect

  • Name, email, and basic profile info during signup
  • Band or musician preferences and activity history
  • Optional media uploads (e.g., images, audio clips)

How We Use Your Data

We use your information to personalize your experience, connect you with musicians and bands, and enhance app features. Data is never sold or shared with third parties without consent.

How to Delete Your Account

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data by emailing us at: support@thejhund.com
Please include the email address or username associated with your account.

Once verified, we will delete your account and all data within 7 business days. You will receive a confirmation email once the process is complete.

Additional Data Deletion Requests

You may also contact us to request deletion of specific data such as profile photos, recordings, or messages.

Data Retention

If no deletion request is received, we retain user data for as long as the account is active. Inactive data may be securely archived or deleted after 90 days.
